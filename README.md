📊 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

🎯 Project Objective
To analyze e-commerce sales data and understand business performance including sales, profit, customer behavior, and product trends.

---

🧠 Business Problem
E-commerce companies need insights on:
- Which products generate maximum revenue
- Customer buying behavior
- Profit vs discount impact
- Regional performance

---

🛠 Tools Used
- SQL
- Power BI
- Excel

---

📂 Dataset Information
The dataset includes:
- Orders
- Products
- Customers
- Sales
- Profit
- Discounts
- Regions

---

📊 Key Metrics
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Order Count
- Category Performance
- Customer Segments

---

📈 Key Insights
- Identified top-selling products
- Found seasonal sales trends
- High revenue regions identified
- Low-profit products due to discounts

------

📌 Impact
This project helps improve:
- Profit optimization
- Customer targeting
- Product strategy
- Business decision-making
